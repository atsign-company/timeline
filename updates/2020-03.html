The @ Company Update - March 2020

It is definitely some difficult times with Covid-19 and all its ramifications. First, we hope you and your family and friends are all safe and well during this difficult time.  We are fortunate in that we designed our business with a virtual office. We did this so that we could attract the best talent that we know, no matter where they are located. So the locking down of the country has had no impact on our business for now. We are still driving hard, but we just have a few more people in our homes!

Website

We have a new website look that is more fun and playful which should go live this week.

Registrar

It is up! Our registrar site is up and running. We are just finishing the testing and then we will open it to those who reserved their @sign. Around mid April we will send out some invitations so that other people can start signing up for their @signs. The site will be invitation-only while it is in Beta.

Fundraising and Development

We are very pleased to announce a partnership with Renascence Development. Renascence will be helping us build out the core technology. We now have a team of four full time plus two shadow developers in addition to @colin and @kevin coding.They are also investing in our SAFE round. They have a great team of core technology developers that will help build out our SDKs, to make it really easy for developers to support the @ protocol.

Technology

We have built out a Kubernetes cluster in Google's cloud to host the registrar website and have been working with SunStoneDefence to provide both security and “are you a human” capability that is way more fun than identifying shop fronts or traffic lights. We have built a web server that can speak the @ protocol on the backend to query in realtime public data held on @servers, this is the basis of the FindMe web service.

We have also added another verb and set of tools to the @protocol which allows public private key authentication and encryption of application data. The pkam: verb will be the verb used by applications speaking to @ servers so was a very important addition to the protocol.

Sources of Revenue

There are many ways to monetize the ecosystem that we are creating, so we laid out our plan and all the different options in this document. If you want to understand more about our go to market, please see this document. As always, we welcome comments and input.

Apps Status

We are building some apps and we have some outside developers working on apps. We will be paying commissions to developers if people buy their @signs through their app.

  • The Find Me app, will probably be the first app available. This app will let you set up your public information and show it on our website or embed it on your own site.
  • We are also creating a special version of the Find Me app for influencers, that will let them consolidate all their brands and their offerings in one place.
  • The Julz Vault app may be the second one available. This is the app that allows executives to share sensitive data with their executive admins safely.
  • The Buzz app, a new version of contacts and groups will take a little longer, but we are all looking forward to that one!
  • LifeSite - A digital vault app for sensitive documents, currently selling b-b-c will be extended to work with the @ protocol.

We hope everyone stays safe!
